These Surrey Forestry hardwood kiln-dried logs are dried in our biomass system. Kiln-dried logs burn longer and hotter than seasoned wood and they are ideal for open fires, chimineas and wood-burning stoves.

Our kiln-dried logs have been naturally air-dried first for at least one year before going into the kiln. Our kiln-dried logs are of the highest quality. We use our own kiln to dry them to complete the drying process until they are less than 15% moisture content.

A loose load is 100 logs. The wood is all local hardwood. The minimum order is two loads.

The minimum order for delivery within a 5-mile radius of Effingham is 200 logs and the delivery charge is £10.

500 plus logs ordered for delivery within 5 miles of Effingham will not be subject to a delivery charge.

Outside of this area of the minimum order is 500 logs plus a delivery charge of £2.00 per mile outside a 5-mile radius of Effingham. Please press buy now to place this order and then we will be in touch to arrange your delivery cost and payment.
